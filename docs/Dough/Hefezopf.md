
# Hefezopf

## :salt: Ingredients
| How much  | What |
| ------- |----------- |
| **1 pack**  | yeast       |
| **190 ml**  | warm milk   |
| **380 g**   | flour       |
| **1 pinch** | salt        |
| **50 g**    | sugar       |
| **1**       | egg         |
| **50 g**    | soft butter |




## :pencil: Instructions
Activate 1 pack yeast in 190 ml warm milk until foamy. Usually this takes 15 min.

For the dough combine 380 g flour with 1 pinch salt of salt.
Add 50 g sugar and the milk.

Knead the dough for 10 min on medium high.

After the dough is coherent but dry, add the 1 egg and the 50 g soft butter.

Knead for an additional 5 min until a soft plyable dough has formed.

Let rise for 1.5 h.

Separate into three equal sized pieced, kneat them well and form the load by braiding the dough.

Brush the loaf with milk and let rise for another 45 min.

Brush the loaf another time with milk and bake at 170&deg;C for 25 min until brown.
