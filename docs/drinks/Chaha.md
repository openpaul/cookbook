
# Chaha

## :salt: Ingredients
| How much  | What |
| ------- |------------- |
| **5 g**     | ginger        |
| **2**       | cardamon pods |
| **600 ml**  | water         |
| **2.5 tsp** | black tea     |
| **3 tsp**   | sugar         |
| **150 ml**  | milk          |




## :pencil: Instructions
For 2-3 cups of chaha thinly slice or grate 5 g ginger.
Optionally you can crush 2 cardamon pods.
Add the spices to 600 ml water and bring to a nice boil.

Simmer the spices until the water has turned slighly green, this can take 3-5 min.

With the water simmering add 2.5 tsp black tea and boild for 2 min.

Add 2-3 tsp sugar and 150 ml milk. Ideally the milk should be room temperature.
Stir to dissolve the sugar and **carefully** bring to a boil twice. This should not take longer than 2 min.

Use a strainer and serve into 2-3 mugs.
